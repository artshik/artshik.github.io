THE FOUNDERS

Grey Matter was founded by MacEagon Voyce, Sam Eckmeier, and Arthur Shikhman. We believe that creating direct lines of revenue from users to artists while giving users stake in the game and an outlet for creative expression can tap into the market overlap between the music industry and social media platforms.
